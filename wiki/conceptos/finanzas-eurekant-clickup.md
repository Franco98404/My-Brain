# Finanzas en ClickUp

Cómo [[eurekant]] trackea finanzas. Space Finance → única lista **Transactions** (`901401372867`), 100+ registros sincronizados con Mercury. Complementa a [[automatizacion-mercury-clickup]].

## Campos por transacción

ARS, USD, Exchange Rate USD/ARS, USD Total (fórmula), **Category** (Income, Payroll, Taxes/Commissions, Software & Tools, Marketing, Company Related, Personal Expenses, Expenses of others), **Payment Method** (Stripe, Bank Transfer, Credit/Debit Card, Cash, Crypto), Transaction Date, **Mercury Transaction ID**, relación a Employee, Attachments (comprobantes). Statuses: paid, pending, recurring. Tag `mercury-sync` para la integración automática.

## Tipos de gasto observados

Nómina mensual a contractors, suscripciones de software (Claude/Claude Pro, ClickUp, GitHub Teams, 1Password, Hostinger, Spark Mail, Google Cloud, FlutterFlow, Apple Developer), marketing (Meta Ads, Meta Verified), hardware (SSD, RAM), telefonía (Claro), e ingresos.
